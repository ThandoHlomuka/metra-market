Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Metra Market

Orders & Shipping

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can also track your order from your profile page under "Order History". Our shipping partner Bobgo provides real-time tracking updates.

What are the shipping costs?

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your delivery address and order weight. We partner with Bobgo Delivery Services. Free shipping may be offered on orders above a certain threshold.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary by location: Major centers (2-5 business days), Regional areas (5-10 business days), Remote areas (10-15 business days).

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within South Africa. International shipping may be available in the future.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in original packaging. Items must be returned with all tags and original receipt. See our Returns Policy page for full details.

How long do refunds take?

Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days after we receive and inspect your return. The refund will be issued to your original payment method.

Can I exchange an item?

Yes, we offer exchanges for defective items, wrong items received, or size exchanges. Contact returns@metramarket.co.za to request an exchange.

Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Credit/Debit Cards, Instant EFT, and Bank Transfer via our secure payment processor PayFast. All payments are encrypted and secure.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes! We use PayFast, a PCI DSS compliant payment processor. Your payment information is encrypted and we never store your card details.

Account

Do I need an account to shop?

Yes, you need to create an account to place orders. This helps us process your orders faster and allows you to track your order history.

How do I reset my password?

Click on the user icon, select "Forgot Password", and enter your email. We'll send you a link to reset your password.

Customer Support

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us at support@metramarket.co.za, call +27 11 123 4567, or use the live chat feature in your profile. We're available Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM SAST.

Do you offer bulk/corporate orders?

Yes! For bulk or corporate orders, please contact corporate@metramarket.co.za for special pricing and arrangements.
